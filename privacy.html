Privacy Policy

Your privacy comes first.

Split Decisions performs all app pairing and launching directly on your Android device.

We do not collect, upload, sell, or share your personal information.

There are no user accounts, no cloud storage, and no remote servers processing your data.

What information does Split Decisions collect?

None.

Split Decisions does not collect your installed apps, usage habits, personal information, contacts, files, or device activity.

Why does the app request access to installed apps?

The QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission is required so Split Decisions can display the applications installed on your device.

This allows you to select two apps and save them as a split-screen pair.

Your installed app list never leaves your device.

Where is my information stored?

Your saved app pairs are stored locally on your device.

HeatingBlanketLabs never receives or stores this information.

Do you use analytics or tracking?

No.

Split Decisions does not include analytics, telemetry, or user tracking.

Do you share information?

No.

Because we never collect your information, there is nothing to sell or share with third parties.

FAQ

Does Split Decisions upload my installed app list?
No. Everything stays on your device.

Why do some apps not open in split-screen?
Some Android apps disable split-screen support. Split Decisions includes instructions for enabling Android's "Force Resizability" option for improved compatibility.

Can HeatingBlanketLabs see what apps I use?
No. We have zero visibility into your device or your installed applications.
